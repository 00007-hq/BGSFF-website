Bangladesh Global Short Film Festival (BGSFF)

About the Festival

The Bangladesh Global Short Film Festival (BGSFF) is an international hybrid short film festival based in Bangladesh. We welcome short films of all genres from filmmakers worldwide. Selected films will be showcased online and at physical venues in Bangladesh.

Categories

  • Ultra Short Film (3–10 Minutes) – All filmmakers
  • Short Film (Up to 15 Minutes) – All filmmakers
  • Bangladeshi Short Film – Only Bangladeshi filmmakers
  • Student Short Film – Only Student filmmakers

Rules & Submission

  • Open to filmmakers from all countries
  • All genres of short films accepted
  • Films must not exceed 15 minutes
  • English subtitles mandatory for non-English films
  • Subtitles must be burned-in
  • Films can be previously screened or premiered
  • Multiple submissions allowed (each film submitted separately)
  • Selected films will be screened online and at physical venues in Bangladesh
  • Bangladeshi filmmakers eligible for Bangladeshi category
  • Student filmmakers must provide valid student ID if selected
